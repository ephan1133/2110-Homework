This is a replication of the World's Hardest Game. The player first enters the start screen where they press START (enter) to continue to the game. 

In the game, there is the player, a spawn point, 2 walls, 5 obstacles, and a goal. The player has 3 lives displayed at the spawn goal. They should maneuver around the obstacles to reach the goal tile without making contact with the obstacles. 

Movement is done with the D-Pad (arrow keys).

Once the game ends, they have the option to press start to go back to the menu and play agian. 

The game can also be reset at any time by pressing SELECT (backspace).
